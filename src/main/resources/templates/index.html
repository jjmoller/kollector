    ABOUT US

    We are a small independent web application development team situated in the Western Cape. With the passion for both card collection and programming, we sought to bring both of these interest into one convenient place. As with a lot of things in life, take all processes and decisions with a grain of salt as this is only a beta form of the proposed web application. We hope you enjoy the use of KOLLECTOR as much as we do.

    OUR PURPOSE

    From the depths of a Stellenbosch programming dungeon, KOLLECTOR was born! This web application serves to give card game enthusiasts a platform to buy, sell and view a wide variety of card game collections. These cards are sold independently by users, for users. We as a team do understand that new cards and sets of cards are constantly innovating and entering the market. This is where the administrator comes in. He or she is in charge of adding, updating and even removing cards and categories of cards. This ensures that the users are constantly up-to-date and equally prepared if and when new sets of cards become available. KOLLECTOR is thus a card marketplace for card enthusiasts, by card enthusiasts.

    This is a BETA version of the full web application. Meaning that some features and design choices are not final. This version ensures that a working backend and frontend web application is realised in due time. Thank you for your understanding.
